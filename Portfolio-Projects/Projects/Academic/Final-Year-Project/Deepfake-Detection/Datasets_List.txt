=============================================================
         MULTIMODAL DEEPFAKE DEFENSE DATASETS
=============================================================
Total Combined Volume: ~55 GB

-------------------------------------------------------------
1. VISION / IMAGE DATASETS (Deepfake Detection)
-------------------------------------------------------------
These datasets consist of highly realistic generated images, GAN 
synthesis, splicing, copy-move forgeries, and authentic photography.

- CASIA V2 (CASIA2)
  Features authentic images and sophisticated copy-move/splicing forgeries.
- CG-1050
  Computer-generated versus photographic image benchmark.
- CIFAKE
  Real and AI-generated synthetic images classification dataset.
- CoMoFoD (small_v2)
  Copy-Move Forgery Dataset focusing on spatial manipulations.
- DETECTAIVSHUMAN
  Modern contrastive dataset pairing highly realistic AI generations against human photography.

-------------------------------------------------------------
2. NLP / TEXT DATASETS (AI Authorship & Linguistics)
-------------------------------------------------------------
These datasets are sourced from HuggingFace to train the sequence 
classification model to detect LLM token probability patterns.

[Verified Human Text Baseline]
- BookCorpus (rojagtap/bookcorpus)
- PG-19 (emozilla/pg19)
- WikiText-103 (Salesforce/wikitext)
- Reddit Title-Body (sentence-transformers/reddit-title-body)

[AI vs. Human Contrastive Sets]
- HC3 (Human ChatGPT Comparison Corpus - Hello-SimpleAI/HC3)
- ArguGPT (SJTU-CL/ArguGPT)
- APT (tasksource/apt)

[Instruction Fine-Tuning & Prompt Response]
- PAWS-X (hgissbkh/paws-x)
- Alpaca (tatsu-lab/alpaca)
- Dolly 15k (databricks/databricks-dolly-15k)

[Adversarial & Edge Cases]
- Real Toxicity Prompts (allenai/real-toxicity-prompts)
